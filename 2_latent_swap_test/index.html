Key Findings

  • 16×/C16 and 32×/C16 show clear disentanglement: keeping spatial retains appearance (21.25/18.42 dB vs GT_A), keeping temporal retains motion.
  • 8×/C4 shows reversed dominance due to limited spatial capacity (only 2 channels), consistent with zero-out results.
  • Swapped results are always much closer to the video whose branch was kept, confirming non-interchangeable information.
