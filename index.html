ARVAE — Supplementary Experiment Results

Disentanglement Verification & Failure Mode Analysis across three model configurations

Exp 1: Zero-Out Test Exp 2: Latent Swap Test Exp 3: Failure Mode Analysis

Overview

We evaluate three ARVAE configurations on the MCL-JCV dataset to verify temporal–spatial disentanglement and analyze failure modes:

  • 8×/C4 — 8× downscale, 2 temporal + 2 spatial channels
  • 16×/C16 — 16× downscale, 2 temporal + 14 spatial channels
  • 32×/C16 — 32× downscale, 2 temporal + 14 spatial channels
