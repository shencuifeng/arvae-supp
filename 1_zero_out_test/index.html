Experiment 1: Latent Zero-Out Test

Independently zero out each branch's latent to quantify unique contribution

Quantitative Results

ModelNormal PSNRZero-TemporalZero-SpatialTemporal DropSpatial Drop
8×/C431.36 dB12.08 dB27.61 dB−19.28 dB−3.75 dB
16×/C1632.67 dB27.11 dB14.24 dB−5.56 dB−18.43 dB
32×/C1627.54 dB24.62 dB15.47 dB−2.92 dB−12.08 dB

Key finding: The two branches carry non-redundant, complementary information. In 8×/C4, the temporal branch dominates; in 16×/C16 and 32×/C16, the spatial branch dominates — governed by channel allocation.
